Privacy Policy

Effective: June 20, 2026 · Last updated: June 20, 2026

CoreTech Labs ("CoreTech", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes what we collect, how we use it, and your rights. It applies to every CoreTech product, service, and website — including coretechlabs.app, our newsletters, intake forms, and email correspondence.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what we need to run our business and serve you:

  • Newsletter email — your email address when you subscribe to our Substack publication (coretechlabs.substack.com).
  • Contact form submissions — name, business name, email, phone, and the message you send us through our Business Butlers intake form.
  • Email correspondence — messages you send to our business inboxes (info@coretechlabs.app, privacy@coretechlabs.app, or any @coretechlabs.app address).

We do not currently run third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or cookies on this site.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver newsletters you've subscribed to.
  • To respond to your inquiries and fulfill service requests.
  • To maintain a record of business correspondence.
  • To improve our products, services, and website.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate. Each has its own privacy practices:

  • Substack — newsletter delivery (coretechlabs.substack.com)
  • AgentMail — business email hosting
  • Cloudflare — DNS, email routing, and web infrastructure
  • GitHub — code repositories and collaboration
  • Vercel — website and application hosting

4. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, or as required by law. Newsletter subscriptions remain until you unsubscribe. Business correspondence is retained for up to 3 years unless legal requirements demand longer.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information.
  • Unsubscribe from newsletters at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@coretechlabs.app.

6. Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your information, including encrypted email transport (TLS), access controls, and reputable service providers. No system is perfectly secure — if you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

7. Children's Privacy

Our products and services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Effective" date at the top reflects when it was last revised. Material changes will be announced via our newsletter or a notice on this site.
